Niche Research and Validation

Start by comparing search demand, competitor quality, content depth, audience spending power, and your ability to produce consistently. Strong automation niches include software tutorials, personal finance explainers, sports history, business documentaries, travel planning, and product research.

Outcome: choose one channel idea with clear audience demand, monetization potential, and manageable production needs.
